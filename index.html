ABOUT US  
30
YEARS  EXPERIENCE
SPECIALIZING IN HIGH-END ROCK DRILLING TOOLS
G.Y HOPH STROVE TOOLS MANUFACTURE CO.,LTD (HOPH), a member of Shareate Group, is primarily focused on producing rock-drilling tools for the mining, construction, tunneling, quarrying, and related industries. HOPH boasts its own factory and laboratory, as well as quality monitoring for high-end rock-drilling tools. In its effort to provide clients with more economical, sturdy, and efficient solutions, HOPH has established an enterprise innovative technology R&D team and an achievement transformation, promotion, and application platform that relies on the "National and Local Joint Engineering Laboratory of High-Performance Metal Structural Materials and Manufacturing Technology" of Guizhou University and the "Guizhou Provincial Key Laboratory of Material Structure and Strength". This team continuously develops key manufacturing technologies for fatigue resistance. The company's core team is comprised of core material experts from Guizhou Province and industrial production technology experts. HOPH inherits the decades of accumulation and technical essence of the Guizhou rock drilling tools industry. Through continuous technological progress, HOPH ensures that product quality remains consistent and in a leading position in the industry.
Committed to the revitalization of China's drilling tool industry
